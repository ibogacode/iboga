Where people work (access)
Patient portal

Clients log in to complete forms, upload EKG/bloodwork, view documents and tapering schedule.

Patient pipeline

Staff see all leads and patients; open a profile to activate forms, move stage, request labs, view documents.

Patient profile

Single place for one patient: forms, onboarding, EKG/bloodwork, tapering, billing, notes, move to management.

Onboarding

Staff view onboarding list and completion status.

Patient management

After ‚Äúmove to management‚Äù: arrival, daily forms, one-time forms, discharge.
